Mom's Banana Bread

1 3/4 c flour
3/4 c sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp baking soda
1 3/4 tsp cream of tartar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 c oil
2 mashed bananas
Mini chocolate chips
Mix dry ingredients, add eggs, oil and banana. Stir well. Mix in chocolate chips (I eyeball it). Put in greased loaf pan. Bake at 350 for 50-60 min or until toothpick in center comes out clean.
